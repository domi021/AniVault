# Feature Ideas

- **Seasonal anime** — browse current season's new and airing shows
- **Airing schedule** — see what episodes air each day
- **Continue watching** — quick resume on the home screen
- **Stats** — total episodes watched, hours, genres breakdown
- **Trailers** — embed YouTube trailers on anime detail page
- **Offline cache** — save search results and lists for offline use
- **Share list** — export your list as text/image
